About workshop

Hey folks!

The Project Management is a platform to learn expert techniques for building successful project teams, creating efficient plans and implementing effective tracking measures to ensure your projects come in on deadline and on budget. In one fast-paced, well-designed day, we’ll cover all the essential elements of project management.

The Project Management is a platform to learn expert techniques for building successful project teams, creating efficient plans and implementing effective tracking measures to ensure your projects come in on deadline and on budget. In one fast-paced, well-designed day, we’ll cover all the essential elements of project management.

To make your weekend amazing, we are organising a two day workshop on the most promising tech of the future-AR/VR. The workshop will bring together mentors, creators, thinkers and tech enthusiasts and help participants develop a deeper understanding of this field and guide them towards developing their skills. The participants will also enjoy some fun activities and mouth watering snacks. So become an AR/VR developer and prepare yourself for future opportunities and challenges. Come and build the future on 4th and 5th April.

About The Speaker

Hi I'm Kartik

I am an AR/VR Evangelist and have been working with immersive technologies for the last 3 years. I am engineer by profession, explorer in mind and an art lover at heart. I am founder of ARVidhya, an Augmented Reality EdTech startup working for making children's education more engaging and fun. I am Winner at Startup Weekend organized by Google for Entrepreneurs held at Microsoft Hyderabad. I recently attended the world's largest AR VR Hackathon held at Massachusetts Institute of Technology, USA. I will be guiding you to learn and understand immersive technologies in detail. We will walk through the development workflow by building different AR apps during the session. I am excited to see you all at the event.
